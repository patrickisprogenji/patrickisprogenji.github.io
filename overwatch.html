Info

Overwatch is a team-based, first person shooter where you have to capture or defend the objective. The game was created by Blizzard Entertainment in May of 2016. One team is made of 6 people who are playing as a different hero. To get to capture the point you must eliminate the enemy team by using your basic attack, abilities, and ultimate moves. You must work with your team to create a good team composition to win.

Heros

There are 4 brackets that each hero is put in: Attack, Defense, Tank, and Support. The Attack heros include Genji, McCree, Pharah, Reaper, Soilder 76, Sombra, and Tracer. Attack heros excel in attacking enemies in the frontlines and flanking behind the enemies. The Defense heros consist of Bastion, Hanzo, Junkrat, Mei, Torbjorn, and Widowmaker. The Defense heros a great at doing huge amounts of damage from far away to keep themselves from dieing. The Tank heros are based of D.Va, Reinhardt, Roadhog, Winston, and Zarya. Tank heros have the most health and do the most damage making them able to tank on huge hits and still live. Pair them with some supports and the can take out an enemy team, which leads us to our finally bracket. The Support team is made of Ana, Lucio, Mercy, Symmetra, and Zenyatta. The Supports Job is to heal and enchance there teams abilities like by making there damage go up, providing a healing boost, or even making them survive longer with a damage reduction ability. To win you need a balance team of attack, defense, tank, and support heros. All together, they are capable of destroying their enemies.

Overview

This is a really fun game that can help you practice teamwork and strategizing. The gameplay and graphics are really good and the game has a wide variety of people you can play as. It is challenging enough for everyone so they will want to come back for more.
